Agadir City & Souk Tour

Explore the soul of Agadir through culture, history, and local flavors

Begin your tour with dive into the lively energy of Souk El Had, one of the largest traditional markets in Morocco. Browse through colorful spices, handmade crafts, leather goods, and local produce — a true sensory experience.

Enjoy a relaxing boat tour from Marina Agadir, where you can take in stunning coastal views and feel the sea breeze. Cruise along the Atlantic, spot local marine life, and soak up the sun as you unwind on the water. A perfect way to experience the natural beauty of Agadir from a different perspective.

End your day with a delicious Moroccan lunch at a local restaurant. Enjoy dishes like tagine, couscous, and fresh mint tea — all with a view of the city or ocean.
