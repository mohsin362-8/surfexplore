Timlalin Dunes & Sunset Experience & sand bording

Explore the beauty of the Timlalin Dunes

Discover the beauty of the Timlalin Dunes, where the golden sands stretch endlessly toward the horizon. Perfect for a group outing, you can walk barefoot on the soft dunes, enjoy the peaceful atmosphere, and take stunning photos with sweeping desert landscapes. It's a unique place where the sand meets the sea, creating a magical setting for exploration.

Get your adrenaline pumping with a fun session of sandboarding down the steep dunes! Whether you’re a beginner or looking for a thrilling ride, sandboarding is a must-try group activity. Laugh with friends as you race down the hills or take turns mastering your balance — it's a memory-making experience like no other.

As the sun begins to set, gather with your group to watch the sky transform into a canvas of warm colors. The Timlalin Dunes are famous for their incredible sunset views, where the golden light reflects off the sand, creating an unforgettable and peaceful moment. Bring your camera — this is a sunset you’ll want to remember.

End the adventure with a relaxing break. Many tours offer traditional Moroccan mint tea served right in the dunes, allowing your group to unwind and chat while enjoying the views. Sit in the sand, sip your tea, and feel the gentle desert breeze as the stars begin to appear — a perfect way to end a magical day.
